None of the five patients with antibodies or immunoreactivity displayed any feature of autoimmune epilepsy.We conclude that indiscriminate testing in patients presenting to a first seizure clinic with new-onset seizures or epilepsy is unlikely to improve detection of autoimmune epilepsy.© 2019 The Authors.
