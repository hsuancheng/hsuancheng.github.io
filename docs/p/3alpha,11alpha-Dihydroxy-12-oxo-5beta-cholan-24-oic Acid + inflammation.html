Role of gut metabolism of adrenal corticosteroids and hypertension: clues gut-cleansing antibiotics give us.

Intestinal bacteria can metabolize sterols, bile , steroid hormones, dietary proteins, fiber, foodstuffs, and short chain fatty . The metabolic products generated by some of these intestinal bacteria have been linked to a number of systemic diseases including with Type 2 diabetes mellitus, some forms of inflammation, and more recently, systemic hypertension. In this review, we primarily focus on the potential role selected gut bacteria play in metabolizing the endogenous glucocorticoids corticosterone and cortisol.
